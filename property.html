Description

A beautifully modernised three bedroom detached family home in a quiet child friendly street in the popular St.Andrew's Rise development. The property is presented in a fresh modern style and has double glazing and gas central heating.
The accommodation comprises reception hallway, formal lounge with space for dining, family room in the former garage, fitted kitchen, downstairs WC. Upstairs is the master bedroom with en suite shower, two further bedrooms and a family bathroom. Outside, there is driveway parking and a child friendly garden.
